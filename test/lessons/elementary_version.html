Native plants in the Indigenous Garden

The goal of the garden project is to teach people in the community about Indigenous land teachings as well as bring together Indigenous Leaders, educators, students and members of the community to help research Indigenous studies. Andrew Judge planted foods and plants native to the local area such as wild plums, blackberries, saskatoon berries, paw paws, hazelnuts, sunflowers, artichokes and many more.
